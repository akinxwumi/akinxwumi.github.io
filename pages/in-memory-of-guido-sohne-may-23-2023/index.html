In memory of Guido Sohne - May 23, 2023

Guido Sohne is one of my heroes and would have been 50 today.

Guido’s remarkable achievements and dedication continue to inspire me. In this post For Guido, or requiem for a true tiger, the author perfectly captures Guido’s impact, stating, ‘Here’s a guy who founded companies, co-founded NGOs, and advised international foundations, all with the noble purpose of bringing the promise of technology and innovation to those in his home who were less fortunate than he was. He was the living, breathing embodiment of everything Thomas Friedman talks about in his book ‘The World Is Flat.’ Guido’s life was devoted to nothing less than transforming Africa.’

These words truly encapsulate the greatness that defined Guido Sohne. In honor of his memory, I have curated a catalog of his remarkable achievements, digital memories, and commemorations. Through some of the stories shared by those who knew him intimately, I have gained a deeper understanding of his incredible legacy. I invite you to explore the following accounts, which will undoubtedly illuminate the profound impact Guido had on countless lives. May his extraordinary journey continue to inspire us all.

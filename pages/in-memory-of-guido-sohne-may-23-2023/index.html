In memory of Guido Sohne - May 23, 2023

Guido Sohne, one of my heroes, would have turned 50 today. His remarkable achievements and dedication continue to inspire me. The author of the blog post titled ”For Guido, or Requiem for a True Tiger,” which you can find below, perfectly captures Guido’s impact, stating, “Here’s a guy who founded companies, co-founded NGOs, and advised international foundations, all with the noble purpose of bringing the promise of technology and innovation to those in his home who were less fortunate than he was. He was the living, breathing embodiment of everything Thomas Friedman talks about in his book ‘The World Is Flat.’ Guido’s life was devoted to nothing less than transforming Africa.”

These words truly encapsulate the greatness that defined Guido Sohne. In honor of his memory, I will continue to curate a catalog of his remarkable achievements, digital memories, and commemorations on this page.

When the British Raj asked the Indias to kill poinsonous Cobras, it gave rise to the cobra effect.

The Proof of funds is another of such policies as well. It has been completely hacked at least in Nigeria and I wouldn’t blame anyone who fools the British government this way. It is the path of least resistance and as we know, we are naturally configured to follow this path.

How do the UK government expect lower/middle-class citizens to pony up this exorbitant sum of money? The only way is to either steal it or lie about having it. Good luck with finding out.

I will propose that the UK, Canadian government, etc. instead create a system that encourages people to study but also take into cognizance that they won’t be able to instantly fund their studies. Afte rall, they have a credit system for this purpose specifically in their country for their citizens. Why not extend something similar to academic migrants? They will most likely create more value in your economy anyway as let us be honest, most people japaing right now don’t intend to come back at least not immediately. I should note that there are scholarship programmes but these are not robust enough to accommodate for people that are not in the tail-end of the distribution.

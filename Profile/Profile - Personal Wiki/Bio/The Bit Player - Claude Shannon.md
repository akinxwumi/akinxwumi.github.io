# The Bit Player - Claude Shannon

[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JP1Ljp8X6hg](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JP1Ljp8X6hg)

In case you haven’t seen it, this documentary [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JP1Ljp8X6hg](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JP1Ljp8X6hg) on Claude Shannon is pretty cool. I particularly find his Theseus experiment at Bell’s Labs [https://youtu.be/_9_AEVQ_p74](https://youtu.be/_9_AEVQ_p74) ****very instructive, especially the ending where he showed that even though the electronic mouse is capable of learning to find the correct path in the maze via trial and error, it will become incapable if obstructed. This resonates with me as you can compare a lot of our lives as Africans to that demonstration: it is impediments (mostly artificial) that are preventing us from achieving our maximum potential and not a lack of inherent ability to learn.

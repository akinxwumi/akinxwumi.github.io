# Lessons from Jao

- One of the lessons I learned from Jao - there is usually a silver lining in seemingly challenging problems, disadvantages, or setbacks. I once asked him how he coped with how people perceived him due to his average body size as a man, especially when he was my age, and his response was simply, "Look at me now at my age and compare to my peers; which body will you prefer?” With this question,  he made me tacitly realize the value of biological age over chronological age.
- He was the first to point out that I will do what is in my mind regardless of external conditions (pressures).
